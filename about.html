About JPS Engineering

Delivering precision engineering solutions since our inception.

Company Profile

**JPS Engineering Material Pvt Ltd** is a premier manufacturer and supplier of Engineering Plastics and Machined Components. Established with a vision to provide world-class quality, we specialize in **Cast Nylon (All Grades), POM (Delrin), PEEK, PTFE (Teflon), Polyurethane (PU), and High-Performance Machined Parts**.

Our state-of-the-art facility in **Mohan Nagar Ghaziabad** is equipped with advanced machinery to deliver precision components that meet rigorous international standards. We serve key industries including **Automotive, Textile, Food Processing, Chemical, and Heavy Engineering**.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Quality
  • Custom Machining & Fabrication
  • Rapid Delivery & Competitive Pricing

Quality Policy

"We are committed to satisfying our customers by providing high-quality Engineering Plastic Products and Machined Components on time and at competitive prices. We strive for continual improvement in our Quality Management System and processes."

Our Vision

To become a global leader in the manufacturing of High-Performance Engineering Plastics and to set new benchmarks in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide innovative material solutions that enhance the performance and durability of our customers' machinery. We believe in building long-term partnerships through trust and excellence.
